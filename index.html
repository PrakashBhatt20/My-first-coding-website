Computer works on a basic principle
of IPOS. In IPOS I refers to Input , P
refers to Process , O refers to Output
and S refers to Storage.




Now I am going to define IPOS :
  • Input : Input is an act of putting raw data into the computer.
  • Process : Process is the series of action done by computer to convert raw data into meaningful result.
  • Output : Output is the result shown in output devices after converting raw data into meaningful result.


This was all about basic principle of computer . Now I would like to talk about features of computer
They are :-
  • Accuracy :- The capacity of providing result to user without any errors is called accuracy.
  • Speed :- The capacity of solving problems or equation with minimum time is called Speed.
  • Automation :- Automation is the application of technology , robotics , programs or processes to achieve outcomes with minimal human inputs.
  • Storage :- The capacity of storing high amount of data in computer is called storage.
  • Verstality :- The capacity of doing multiple task at a same time is called verstality.
  • Dilligency :- The capacity of working for a long time without getting tired or bored is called dilligency.
